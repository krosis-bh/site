About

Hi everyone! I'm krosisbh, and this is my personal website where I will post varying stuff just to get stuff out.

I am a graduate student in physics who enjoys personal programming on the side, and I am aiming to do computational physics as a career path. If the font or color scheme of this website doesn't give it away, I am also a Linux daily driver (Debian 12). Stuff related to this will be in stem_stuff.

Like most people these days, I enjoy video games, but while I play a wide variety of games, I enjoy rambling about the relatively niche genres of rhythm games and shoot 'em ups. You can find stuff about that in game_stuff.

Finally, I particularly enjoy language learning, not just programming langauges, but human languages. While I can only confidentally say I am fluent in English, I am very solid at Japanese, and am able to ask for directions in German. However, I do want to learn as many languages as possible in my lifetime. Stuff related to this will be in language_stuff.

Miscellaious stuff will be in other_stuff.

The font of this website is Iosevka, and the color scheme is based off Kanagawa. This website tries to be as plain html and css as possible, but I am using MathJax so I can render equations. \( \frac{\partial L}{\partial q} = \frac{d}{dt} \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}}\)
